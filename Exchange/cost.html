Flights:
Leeds - Heathrow - Chicago - Madison $1000 (this was booked rather late however)

Acquiring Visa:
~ $200 (including Sevis Fee, Visa application fee and transport to London)

Health Insurance:
SHIP $1300 (Student Health Insurance Plan - provided by the UW Univesity Health Service)

Accommadation:
Dejope $8000 (including a fee to reduce prices of food in residence hall eateries)

Tuition Fees:
Half the normal Warwick Fee ~$1500 (No tuition is paid to UW)

Athletic Facilities:
$0 (access to all gyms, swimming pools and running tracks are free)

Text books
~$100 (this would have been far more, but abebooks.com and other such sites allow you to find even obscure titles for lower prices)


If you are elligible for Student Finance support, you can apply for a grant that will reimburse you for travel, visa and health insurance costs.
